Proud of where we're from and sharing local news

Welcome to Leitrim Today, where users registered on this site can post updates of news and events from around the county.
The information contained here will allow visitors and holiday makers to discover local points of interest as they visit County Leitrim.
I grew up in a rural area near Dromahair in North County Leitrim and I have always had a love for my home county, the image to the right shows the view from the hill behind my family home.
I walked this hill most mornings and evenings as a teenager checking on the cattle for my father. There is no way to describe the feeling of being on a windswept hill in wind swept or stormy weather feeling that connection to nature. I have spent far too many years away from my home, my family and friends, and hope to reconnect in the coming years.
